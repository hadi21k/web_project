Why Reading

This AI guide provides an overview of what AI is, its impact on society, and its inner workings, including key concepts and principles such as machine learning and neural networks. It explores Image Generators, such as GANs and style transfer, and their applications. The guide also covers the future potential of AI, its uses in different industries, and the job opportunities available in the field. Whether you're a tech enthusiast, student, or just curious about AI, this guide offers an informative look at this exciting field.

Common examples of AI

Virtual Personal Assistants (e.g. Siri, Alexa) - These are AI-powered digital assistants that can answer questions, perform tasks, and provide recommendations.

Self-driving Cars - AI technologies such as computer vision, machine learning, and robotics are used to develop self-driving cars that can safely navigate roads and make decisions in real-time.

Chatbots - AI-powered chatbots are used to provide customer service and support, handling a wide range of inquiries and interactions with users.
