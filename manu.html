What is AI in manufacturing?

AI in manufacturing involves using technology to automate complex tasks and unearthing previously unknown patterns in manufacturing processes or workflows.

How is AI used in the manufacturing industry?

In manufacturing, AI can offer the most value in planning and production floor operations. According to a BCG report, the most important AI use cases in the manufacturing industry are:

  • Intelligent, self-optimizing machines that automate production processes
  • Forecasting efficiency losses for better planning.
  • Detecting quality defects to facilitate predictive maintenance.

Introduction to Predictive Maintenance

Predictive maintenance is a proactive maintenance strategy that uses machine learning and other data-driven methods to predict when equipment is likely to fail. The goal of predictive maintenance is to prevent equipment failures before they occur, reducing downtime and maintenance costs, and improving overall equipment performance and reliability.

Use Cases

Predictive maintenance is used to monitor the condition of pumps in various industrial applications, such as oil and gas, water treatment, and chemical processing. Vibration data from pumps is analyzed using machine learning algorithms to detect signs of wear and tear and predict when maintenance is required.
Predictive maintenance can monitor the condition of motors in industrial applications, such as manufacturing, mining, and power generation. The electrical health of motors is monitored using Motor Current Signature Analysis (MCSA) to detect issues and prevent them from becoming serious problems.
Predictive maintenance can monitor the condition of bearings in various industrial applications, such as automotive manufacturing, wind turbines, and material handling. Vibration analysis is used to detect issues such as misalignment, looseness, and wear in bearings.
In the aerospace industry, predictive maintenance is used to monitor the health of aircraft systems, such as engines, landing gear, and avionics. Sensor data is analyzed to predict maintenance needs and reduce the risk of unplanned downtime while improving safety.
